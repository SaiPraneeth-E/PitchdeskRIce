What We Do

India’s first AgriTech super app + rural franchise network

RICE is building India's first comprehensive AgriTech ecosystem designed to empower smallholder farmers throughout their entire agricultural lifecycle. We bridge the gap between technology and on-ground rural realities.

Our Core Offerings:

  • Hyperlocal RICE Centers: Physical franchise-operated hubs providing access to all RICE services, building trust and local support.
  • Data-Driven Crop Planning: AI-powered advisory for optimal crop selection, sowing times, and practices based on local data.
  • Smart Farming Inputs & Training: Access to quality-assured seeds, fertilizers, and equipment, coupled with skill development via our "Skill Lab".
  • Micro-Warehousing & Cold Storage: A network of accessible storage facilities to prevent post-harvest losses and enable better price realization.
  • Profitable Market Linkages: Direct connections to B2B buyers and consumers, bypassing exploitative middlemen and ensuring fair prices.
  • Embedded Financial Services: Facilitating input credit and other financial products tailored to agricultural cycles.

By integrating these services, RICE offers a holistic "AgriOS" (Agricultural Operating System) that helps farmers plan better, grow smarter, store safely, and sell profitably.

The Problem

India’s 100M+ small farmers face a systemic gap

Smallholder farmers, the backbone of Indian agriculture, are trapped in a cycle of low productivity and income due to several interconnected challenges:

  • No data-backed crop planning: Decisions often based on tradition or speculation, leading to market gluts or unsuitable crop choices.
  • No reliable access to certified inputs: Proliferation of spurious or low-quality seeds, fertilizers, and pesticides.
  • No local storage — forced distress selling: Lack of accessible and affordable post-harvest storage compels farmers to sell produce immediately at low prices.
  • No direct market access — middlemen dominate: Complex supply chains with multiple intermediaries absorb a significant portion of the final value (often 60-70%).
  • No accessible credit tied to actual output: Limited access to formal credit, often relying on informal lenders with high interest rates. Existing credit isn't always aligned with farming cycles.

The Crux: Fragmented solutions have consistently failed rural Bharat. Standalone apps, isolated warehousing projects, or pure marketplace platforms don't address the systemic, interconnected nature of these problems. Farmers need a trusted, holistic partner.

Our Solution – The RICE Stack

An integrated, rural-first platform: Tech + Infra + Trust

The RICE Stack is a comprehensive, six-layered solution designed as a full Agricultural Operating System (AgriOS) for Bharat's small farmers:

  • 🧠 Smart Crop Planner: AI and data science-driven tool providing personalized recommendations on what to grow, when to grow, and how to grow, considering soil health, weather patterns, market demand, and water availability.
  • 💸 Input Credit (Pay-after-Harvest): Facilitating access to quality agri-inputs (seeds, nutrients) on credit, with repayment aligned with the harvest cycle, reducing upfront financial burden.
  • 📦 Micro-Warehousing-as-a-Service: Establishing a distributed network of small-scale, modern storage units (including cold storage where needed) within a 5-10km radius of farm clusters, run by local franchisees.
  • 📱 Skill Lab via WhatsApp/IVR: Delivering crucial agricultural knowledge, best practices, and training modules through accessible channels like WhatsApp and Interactive Voice Response, overcoming literacy and digital barriers.
  • 🛒 B2B Agri Marketplace: A transparent platform connecting farmers directly to bulk buyers, food processors, and retailers, ensuring better price discovery and reducing intermediaries.
  • 🏪 Franchise-Operated RICE Centers: Hyperlocal physical touchpoints run by trained rural entrepreneurs. These centers act as the delivery node for all RICE services, building community trust and providing last-mile support.

The Synergy: By combining cutting-edge Tech (AI, apps), essential Infrastructure (warehousing, RICE centers), and deep-rooted community Trust (local franchisees, field officers), RICE delivers a powerful, end-to-end solution that truly empowers farmers.

Farmer Example – Real Impact Flow

Transforming Ravi's Farming Journey

Let's follow Ravi, a small farmer, and see how RICE transforms his agricultural cycle:

  • Joins RICE: Ravi is onboarded by a local RICE field officer or visits a nearby RICE Center. His farm details and historical data are logged.
  • Smart Planning: The RICE Planner, considering Ravi's land, local conditions, and market trends, recommends cultivating high-demand tomatoes instead of his usual low-margin crop.
  • Input on Credit: Ravi avails quality tomato seeds and organic nutrients worth ₹5,000 through RICE's input credit facility, with payment deferred until after harvest.
  • Skill Enhancement: He receives timely advice and learns best practices for tomato cultivation via interactive WhatsApp modules from the RICE Skill Lab.
  • Secure Storage: At harvest, Ravi stores his 2 tons of tomatoes at the local RICE micro-cold storage unit, preventing spoilage and allowing him to wait for better market prices.
  • Profitable Sale: RICE's B2B marketplace connects Ravi with a bulk buyer offering a fair price. His produce is sold for ₹60,000.
  • Seamless Settlement: The input credit of ₹5,000 is automatically deducted, and Ravi receives a payout of ₹55,000 directly to his account – a significant increase from his previous earnings.

Key Outcomes for Ravi:

✅ Higher Income: Substantially increased profits due to better crop choice, reduced losses, and fair pricing.
✅ Better Decisions: Empowered by data and knowledge to make informed choices at every stage.
✅ Reduced Risk: Minimized post-harvest losses and protection from price volatility.
✅ Rural Dignity Restored: Access to modern tools, fair treatment, and recognition as a key contributor to the food system.

Business Model

Multi-Point Monetization for Sustainable Growth

RICE employs a diversified revenue model, capturing value at multiple points in the agricultural ecosystem. This ensures financial sustainability and aligns our success with farmer prosperity.

Revenue Stream Description Monetization Metric
Input Sales Margin Sale of certified seeds, fertilizers, nutrients, and vertical farming kits through RICE Centers and app. Margin on input products (e.g., 10-15%)
Storage Subscription / Fee Fees for using RICE micro-warehousing and cold storage facilities. ₹ per ton/month or per crate/day
B2B Trade Commission Commission on successful trades facilitated through the RICE B2B marketplace connecting farmers to buyers. Percentage of transaction value (e.g., 2-5%)
Credit Facilitation Fee Fees from NBFCs/Banks for sourcing, underwriting support, and servicing agri-loans (input credit, equipment finance). Percentage of loan amount or service fee
Franchise Fee & Revenue Share One-time setup fee for RICE Center franchisees and ongoing revenue sharing agreement. Rural entrepreneurs (franchisees) retain 60–75% of local operational profits. Upfront fee + % of RICE Center revenue
Data Analytics & Advisory (Future) Premium data insights and specialized advisory services for larger agri-businesses or institutions. Subscription or project-based fees

This multi-pronged approach creates a robust financial foundation, allowing RICE to scale its impact while providing affordable and valuable services to farmers.

Why Now? (Market Timing)

  • Bharat is Digitally Ready: Rapid adoption of smartphones, UPI for digital payments, and widespread use of WhatsApp/IVR even in rural areas provide the rails for tech-enabled solutions.

RICE's Unique Position: While many AgriTech players focus on specific niches, RICE's model of combining Tech + Infrastructure + Community-based Scale (via franchise RICE Centers) directly addresses the execution gap.
